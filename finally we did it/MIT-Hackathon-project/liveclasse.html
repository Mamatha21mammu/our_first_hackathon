Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I join a live class?

To join a live class, simply navigate to the class page and click the "Join" button when the class is scheduled to start. You’ll be redirected to the Zoom meeting or video conferencing platform used for the session.

2. Do I need an account to join the live class?

No, you do not need to create an account to join a live class. However, you will need the meeting link or ID, which will be provided to you by the course administrator or instructor.

3. Can I participate in the live class if I am late?

Yes, you can join a live class at any time. However, we recommend joining as early as possible to avoid missing important information and instructions.

4. What if I encounter technical difficulties during the class?

If you experience technical issues during the live class, such as audio or video problems, please check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, you can contact the support team via the support chat on the website.

5. Can I ask questions during the live session?

Yes, most instructors allow participants to ask questions during the class using the chat feature or via audio. If you want to ask a question verbally, please wait for the instructor to call on you.

6. How do I access the class recording after the session?

After the live class, the recording will be made available on the course platform. You will receive an email notification with a link to the recording, or you can access it through your account dashboard.
